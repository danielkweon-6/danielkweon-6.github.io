Game Mechanics

Tumble offers a distinctive puzzle experience, characterized by a dynamic game board that rotates as tiles cascade downwards, merging seamlessly. Strategize your moves to achieve the highest score by skillfully combining tiles and earning points with each successful merge.

How to Play

  • Rotate the board using a drag gesture, either clockwise or counterclockwise.
  • Watch as tiles cascade downwards, settling at the bottom of the board.
  • Tiles with the same numbers combine upon collision, merging into larger tiles.
  • Earn points with each successful merge. Larger merges yield larger points adding to your score.
  • The game concludes when the board reaches its maximum capacity, leaving no available moves to continue.

Tips for Success

  • Plan your moves carefully to create large merges.
  • Keep an eye on potential tile placements to strategize effectively.
  • Merge your tiles when possible to prevent your board from filling up.
  • Remember, you can only undo your most recent move.
  • Points are only collected after the game is over.

Unlockables

Use points earned in the game to unlock new challenging grids and vibrant colors. Customize your gameplay experience with a variety of options.
