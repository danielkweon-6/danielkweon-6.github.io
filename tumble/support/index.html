Tumble — Support

Your go-to place for help and guidance on Tumble.

Game mechanics

Tumble is a puzzle game with a dynamic board that rotates as tiles cascade and merge. Strategize to achieve the highest score by combining tiles; each merge earns points.

How to play

  • Rotate the board by dragging clockwise or counterclockwise.
  • Tiles cascade downward and settle at the bottom.
  • Tiles with the same number combine on collision into larger tiles.
  • Earn points with each merge; larger merges yield more points.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no moves remain.

Tips

  • Plan moves to create large merges.
  • Watch potential tile placements to strategize.
  • Merge when possible to avoid filling the board.
  • You can only undo your most recent move.
  • Points are tallied when the game is over.

Unlockables

Use points earned or purchased in the game to unlock new grids and color themes and customize your experience.
