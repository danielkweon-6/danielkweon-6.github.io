Game Mechanics

Number Hunt offers a unique puzzle experience where you navigate a grid of numbers and operators. Your goal is to form expressions that match the target number by tracing paths through the grid. Each puzzle challenges your problem-solving and arithmetic skills, offering fresh challenges with every playthrough.

How to Play

  • Start by selecting a starting point on the grid.
  • Trace a path by moving horizontally or vertically through the grid to connect numbers and operators.
  • Use arithmetic operations to form expressions that match the target number.
  • The game ends when you successfully find an expression that matches the target number or when no valid path is left.

Tips for Success

  • Plan your moves carefully to form valid expressions that lead to the target number.
  • Look for operators and numbers that complement each other to create more complex expressions.
