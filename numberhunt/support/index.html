Number Hunt — Support

Your go-to place for help and guidance on Number Hunt.

Game mechanics

Number Hunt is an arithmetic puzzle. You navigate a grid of numbers and operators to form expressions that match the target number by tracing paths through the grid. Each puzzle challenges problem-solving and arithmetic skills.

How to play

  • Select a starting point on the grid.
  • Trace a path horizontally or vertically to connect numbers and operators.
  • Use arithmetic operations to form expressions that equal the target number.

Tips

  • Plan your moves to form valid expressions that reach the target.
  • Look for operators and numbers that work together for more complex expressions.
  • Try different paths to explore solutions and improve.
  • Challenge yourself by finding longer expressions in shorter time.

Hints

If you're stuck, you can use hints (earned or purchased) to get guidance toward the solution.
